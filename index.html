You are invited!

By: Anyone
To do: Anything
Duration: All time

Although this invite has no time or location, No date and no RSVP it is still very useful. Here are just a few things you could do with it:

  1. Take this down to a disco that wouldn't have you in a million years
  2. Take this to a party all the way across town
  3. Give this to your crying neighbor

We suspect that what you'll discover upon using this invite for these purposes is:

1. You'll flash it once and next thing you know is you'll be inside with a drink. While it might be satisfying to have made it in, you'll likely not stay too long there as you will notice that no one is having much fun.
2. This party will be thrown by the friend of an ex-thing. We recommend you tell her that you are sorry you came. This will give her the opportunity to assure you that... You are invited, much like it says at the top of this invitation. While that should feel nice you'll probably want to leave the party as well since it won't be all that great.
3. Upon arriving home you may notice your neighbor crying on his front porch and when you approach him you'll discover (barely) that he failed to attend a party. This invite at that point will have done you little good so you may as well throw it down at his feet and brief him on some of its potential benefits.
